Alfea College for Faries

o

The Alfea College for Fairies is a girls' boarding school in Magic for Fairies and the most prestigious educational institution for fairies.

Alfea is an ancient school for fairies, and apparently the only one dedicated to fairy education only. The Alfea students are mostly in training ages 16-18.

Fairies here learn how to control their power, fight evil, learn new spells as well as be guardians of their planets, or in many cases, learn how to be their realms' queens.
